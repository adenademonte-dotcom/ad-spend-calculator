10:1 reality check

This model tracks sourced S2 pipeline only — counted when a qualified meeting happens with a real buyer. No influenced pipeline, no view-through credit.

At total spend of $90k, a 10:1 sourced ratio requires $895k in new S2 pipeline — 99% of your entire $900k annual target. That breaks the math.

10:1 is an influenced number. Decision campaign sourced ceiling: LinkedIn 8–12:1 + Google 8–12:1 = blended 9.5:1. That's the number to commit to.
